Privacy Policy

Read applications logs

In order to identify issues the applicaiton write some informations into the devices, the info are stored on logs, because of that the logs request is needed:

  1. android.permission.READ_LOGS

This permission is ignored on latest Android versions.

Mounting and unmounting attached storage

Bellow permission is required to allow mounting and unmounting file systems for removable storage.

  1. android.permission.MOUNT_UNMOUNT_FILESYSTEMS

Non Disclosure

The StorageInfo does not handle personnal or sensitive user data, and no personnal data is captured or exchanged outside the application.
